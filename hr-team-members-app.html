How the training matrix works
  1. A SOP is created in the video system, or a person joins a workflow.
  2. A "watch this SOP" task is assigned to them. Click a + gap to assign it here.
  3. They watch the SOP video and complete the task. It shows as ◔ watching.
  4. On completion they are ticked ✓ trained for that SOP.
  5. The matrix shows who is trained on what and flags the gaps to assign. Linked to Systemisation and Tasks.
